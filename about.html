Mia Jang

I am a UX Designer based in Vancouver, Canada.
My academic background encompasses linguistics and language technology.

Why is my name Mia?

The process of choosing my name:

- Is the name Mia easily transcribable into Korean?
- Can Starbucks baristas spell Mia correctly without hesitation on their first attempt?
- Does Mia have a special meaning in Korean?
- Are there positive associations with the name Mia in other languages?

These considerations were not just about selecting a name.
I wanted to embrace an identity that transcends cultural and linguistic boundaries.
If you are curious about the meanings behind my name, feel free to reach out!

Unique Facts about me

1. My life has been a journey across continents, having resided in Korea (Seoul), Hawaii (Big Island), Canada (Halifax), and India (New Delhi).

2. I have a passion for embracing new cultures, which has led me to study Spanish. I'm proud to say I've maintained a streak of over 200 days on Duolingo.

3. I have a passion for communication, particularly when it involves engaging with children. This includes my sister, who is 12 years younger than me. My enthusiasm for teaching has led me to spend more than six years working as an English tutor.
