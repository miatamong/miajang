AI Chatbot for Hyundai department store @Kakaoenterprise


Project Brief: JellBbo is an AI chat service designed for customers of the Hyundai Department Store. The goal of JellBbo is to enhance the shopping experience. By leveraging artificial intelligence, JellBbo aims to provide timely and accurate support for a wide range of customer inquiries, from product information to customer service issues.

Hyundai Department Store


Hyundai Department Store is a major South Korean retailer renowned for its comprehensive lifestyle offerings both online and offline.




Jellbbo



The chatbot, named JellBbo, is developed to enhance the customer service experience by offering friendly and engaging interactions. During this project‚Äôs development, the system was evaluated with over 25,000 Hyundai Department Store customers, achieving a remarkable 97% accuracy rate in responses.

JellBbo provides personalized, 24/7 customer service, addressing a broad spectrum of inquiries related to sales and services of the Hyundai Department Store and its online platform. It allows customers to access content from the Hyundai Department Store's YouTube channel and STYLE-H magazine. For more complex questions, JellBbo connects customers directly with the call center counselors of Hyundai Department Store, who are available from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m.

The chatbot's AI engine, powered by Kakao Enterprise's 'Advanced Machine Learning', represents cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. This chatbot highlights the technology's effectiveness and its potential to revolutionize customer service in the retail sector.



Availability



This AI chatbot is accessible through various channels including the Hyundai Department Store mobile app, official website, the official KakaoTalk channel, as well as on theHyundai.commobile app and website. Furthermore, customers can engage with JellBbo via'Visible ARS' during call center consultations, enhancing the customer service experience.



Anticipated Advantages



JellBbo's launch is expected to provide substantial benefits to customers by offering convenient access to a wide array of information, such as details on shopping benefits, pop-up stores, new brand launches at Hyundai Department Store locations, and sales events. Features like parking pre-payment, online order tracking, and delivery status checks are designed to enhance the shopping experience. Hyundai Department Store intends to use this technology to innovate in marketing communications and customer service.
